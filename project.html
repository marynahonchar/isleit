Humanity has spent hundreds of years and a lot of money to create a convenient and comfortable megapolises. However, many people are living there only as long as they get an opportunity to move to more environment friendly and quiet place where they can not only work but also live, travel, raise children.

IT professionals are more and more willing to live among others of their own kind, and also, concentrated in one place they gives an incredible synergy, productivity and social impact.

Let's imagine a unique settlement of people connected by the similar ideas, life style, approach to life, education, significant psychological compatibility, and live in one of the socially, economically, medically developed Caribbean/Central America States with perfect ecologically, technically and nature life reputation.

Apartness the settlement does not mean its isolation and secrecy, conversely, except for the special atmosphere in the city is supposed to be fully engaged in ordinary life outside the village.

Assumed the construction of at least 50 homes and the necessary infrastructure on the not less than 5 hectares area, with the possibility for further expansion.
