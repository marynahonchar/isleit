IsleIT team

about us

We are a group of investors and entrepreneurs interested in developing an integrated real estate project on Caribbean.

For more than 15 years, our team has specialized in emerging markets investment, infrastructure projects, IT, project developments.

The main idea is a creation and development of separate and unique city/village. The inhabitants of which will connect by the similar ideas, life style, approach to life, education, significant psychological compatibility, etc.

Nature

Creating a settlement is expected in one of the socially, economically, medically developed Caribbean/Central America States with perfect ecologically, technically and nature life reputation.

IT & Sience Specialists

A key feature of the settlement is the fact that housing is supposed to sell exclusively to representatives of science, IT, and all those who, by their direct activities may promote the development of village and the state as a whole.

Infrastructure

Assumed the construction of at least 50 homes and the necessary infrastructure on the not less than 5 hectares area, with the possibility for further expansion.

Live among your own kind

individuals

We offer not just a “property on island", but complex solution of perfect place for living.

Settlement located in a perfectly suitable place to live, with excellent climate, ecology, health care, social stability, opportunities for further self-realization, extremely attractive tax regime, developed communications and incalculable opportunities for recreation, tourism and education of children.

Social Responsibility

state

Construction of the modern village from 50-100 houses - quite significant project requiring large number of local workers and professionals, building materials, communications.

Such settlement will certainly have a positive impact on the image of the state, business and educational levels, new business links, mutual projects and researches.
